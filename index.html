What you want to know about Quantum

  • Can anyone join quantum?

    Yes anyone above 18 years can join quantum and from anywhere in the world.

  • What is the least amount to use on quantum?

    The least is $10.

  • Do I need to refer people?

    No you don’t need to refer. However if you desire to make more money then you need to constantly refer.

  • Do I pay any fee for using quantum at first?

    When you first sign up you wont pay any fee, however at the end of the month you are required to pay according to your level else you won't be able to trade.

  • What if I don’t trade would I be suspended?

    No you wont. However you won't make more money.

  • If I sell or buy and the buyer or seller does not respond by paying, what do I do?

    You may need to wait for the crypto currency to land in your wallet. Sometimes it takes up to 20 minutes depending on which crypto used in transaction or which exchange the person is using to send you the money. However if the person doesn’t respond , simply contact the support by sending an email to the support with details of transaction of your crypto currency. Send Screen shot of the transaction, your ID number and the person’s ID number you transacted with.

  • If I am supposed to send crypto in exchange for the NFT I am purchasing from the seller and the crypto currency didn’t go or there is network problem what can I do?

    You simply refresh the page.

  • Can I trade on Quantum marketplace without having an exchange wallet?

    You will need to have a wallet with any exchange like Trust wallet or Binance.
